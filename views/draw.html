Draw

Make your own paint by number - made for yourself to paint in or for someone else!

Supplies:

- Canvas or paper

- Marker (black)

- Pencil

- Paint

Steps:

1) Draw a random line going all the way across your canvas from one side to another.

2) Draw some more lines - get crazy! Make a pattern of lines, draw some shapes, make it look abstract.

3) Label all the white spaces (all the spaces you want to be filled with color at least) with a number in pencil.

4) Assign paints to each number so that each number is a unique color.

5) Fill in the spaces!



Draw from reference

Supplies:

- Canvas or paper

- Writing utensils (with color if you want)

Steps:

1) Stand up, close your eyes, and spin around. Stop. First thing you lay eyes on is now your muse.

2) Situate yourself so you’re sitting at an angle you want to draw this object at.

3) Sketch with pencil.

4) Add color.

5) If you're feeling extra funky, add a cool background!



Draw a new species

Supplies:

- Canvas or paper

- Writing utensils (with color if you want)

Steps:

1) Go to your favorite internet browser and search for a random animal generator - generate a random animal.

2) Draw that animal.

3) Now go back to that generator and generate another animal.

4) Erase features of your first animal and redraw them as the other animal - erase your monkey’s feat and give him duck feet.

5) Color it if you want and then come up with a cool new name for your species.
