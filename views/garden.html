Garden

Grow with some seeds

Supplies:

- Seeds

- Soil

- Pots

- Water

Steps:

1) Fill your pot with dirt

2) Dig a little well in the dirt - don’t be afraid to get your hands a little dirty.

3) Place your seeds in the hole.

4) Cover with more dirt.

5) Water! And don’t forget to water every few days.



Grow some vegetables - don’t have seeds? Don’t worry! Just open your fridge - works really well for scallions.

Supplies:

- Scallions

- Glass/cup

- Water

Steps:

1) Grab your scallions and cut those roots off.

2) drop those roots in a glass of enough water to cover them - make sure the roots point down.

3) Now scallions will regrow! Be sure to change the water every couple of days.



Make a fake garden - don’t have any supplies to grow anything real? Make a fun fake garden

Supplies:

- Shoebox

- Art supplies (glue, tape, paper, scissors, pipe cleaners, popsicle sticks)

Steps:

1) Remove the lid off your shoebox - you can use just the bottom half - flip it upside down so you have a flat surface to work with.

2) Cut green paper (you can also paint or color white paper) to look like grass and tape/glue to box.

3) Use pipe cleaners or popsicle sticks and paper to make flowers and plants.

4) Tape or glue those plants to the box.

5) Put your box in the window - it’s just like a window box planter!
