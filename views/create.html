Tie dye - everyone and their mothers are doing it, so can you!

Supplies:

- Fabric dye (from a tie dye kit)

- Rubber bands

- Tarp or some sort of protective covering

- White clothes

Steps:

1) Lay down your protective tarp so you don’t dye your table

2) Take your white article of clothing and soak it in water

3) Wring it out and tie with rubber bands

4) Squirt different color dye in desired sections

5) Let dry, rinse, and wash



Make a playlist - the perfect way to keep your vibe strong through quarantine.

Supplies:

- Music streaming site (Apple Music, Spotify, Youtube - premium or not, doesn’t matter)

Steps:

1) Pick a theme - a big concept (like love, or friendship), weather (like rain or sunny), day of the week, month, animal, mood (like happy, sad)

2) Pick a cover photo - You can pick one from google or even take your own! It can fit your theme or just be something you like.

3) Add songs that fit the theme - add songs you know, add songs you like, listen to some new songs and add those!



Make a mood board - share your dreams, current mood, or anything else your feeling visually.

Supplies:

- Magazines, photos

- Stickers

- Glue

- Tape

- Paper

- Scissors

Steps:

1) Find some photos, phrases or words, colors, images that you like and cut them out

2) Place down a larger piece of paper

3) Lay out the images/photos/words/phrases you like and re-arrange a thousand more times until you find a composition you enjoy - don’t be afraid to layer and overlap

4) Tape or glue your content down

5) Add stickers or glitter or draw over things
